3.10 Person Map

The following Elixir map represents a person structure with named fields:

person = %{ "name" => "Sam", "age" => 31 }

Select the option that shows the results of calling Map.fetch(person, "age") and Map.fetch!(person, "id") respectively.

[Single Answer]

{:ok, 31} and :error
{:ok, 31} and KeyError
31 and KeyError
"31" and 0


Answer: {:ok, 31} and KeyError
