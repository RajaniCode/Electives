3.8 Filter the Var

Which of the following is the correct use of filter_var to validate an email?

[Single Answer]

<?php filter_var($email, FILTER_VALIDATE_EMAIL); ?>
<?php filter_var(FILTER_VALIDATE_EMAIL, $email); ?>
<?php filter_var($email, VALIDATE_EMAIL_VAR); ?>


Answer: <?php filter_var($email, FILTER_VALIDATE_EMAIL); ?>
