4.5 Pagination

Which of the following lines of code will paginate our farms from the controller and display 10 per page?

[Single Answer]

$farms = Farm::all()->paginate(10);
$farms = Farm::orderBy('name', 'asc')->pages(10);
$farms = Farm::orderBy('name', 'asc')->paginate(10);
$farms = Farm::orderBy()->pages(10);


Answer: $farms = Farm::orderBy('name', 'asc')->paginate(10);
