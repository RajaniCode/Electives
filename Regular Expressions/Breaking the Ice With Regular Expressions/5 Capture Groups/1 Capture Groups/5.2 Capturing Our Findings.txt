5.2 Capturing Our Findings

Regex allows us to capture and use values extracted from our subject strings. Which of the following expressions would capture and return the result 'blue' from 'sailing the great blue seas'?

[Single Answer]

sailing the great [\w+] seas
sailing the great <\w+> seas
sailing the great {\w+} seas
sailing the great (\w+) seas


Answer: sailing the great (\w+) seas
